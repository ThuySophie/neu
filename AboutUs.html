About Us

Our Company

Good Nuts is an Australian owned family company established in 1993. Good Nuts is based in Sydney with distribution operations in most states. Originally specialising in complimentary bar snacks, we have recently moved into other lines including confectionery and beverages.

What We Do

Good Nuts currently services over 1,500 leading hotels and clubs around Australia. We specialise in ensuring your customers are enjoying premium quality products that are convenient for them and affordable for you.

‘Good Nuts’ Guaranteed

If within 30 days from your first purchase with us, you are unhappy with any aspect of our products or our service, we’ll refund your money. Our guarantee allows you to trial our products with absolute confidence, secure in the knowledge that if they or our service doesn’t meet your expectation you’ll get your money back.
